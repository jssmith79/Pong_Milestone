How to play Jeff's Modern Pong game...A browser version of a classic!


  • Press ENTER to start a game or the next turn
  • Player 1 - Press "W" to Move Up
  • Player 1 - Press "S" to Move Down
  • Player 2 - Click "Up Arrow" to move up
  • Player 2 - Click "Down Arrow" to move down
